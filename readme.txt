== Description ==

NoCake WP Forms is advanced visual form builder allowing creating complex forms as simple as it can be.
It uses WYSIWYG principle ("What you see is what you get"), so you know how the final form will look
on every step of creation process.

Features:

* Doznes of controls - Out of the box you get several dozen of form controls allowing you to compose even most advanced form.
* What you see is what you get - At every stage of creation you exactly know how your form will look like thanks to visual form editor. It's so easy even your cat may use it!
* All submissions in one place - Sending submissions to your email is not enough. NoCake Forms offers effortless way to manage submissions straight from Wordpress administration panel.
* Exporting & importing forms - You can easly export your forms to another site using exporting & importing functionallity.
* Multiple themes - Choose how your form should look like. With multiple themes delivered out of the box you will always adjust form's appearance to your website.
* Gutenberg ready - Use shortcode or Gutenbergs block, whatever you prefer more.

== Installation ==

* Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/nocake-forms` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
* Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress

You will find **NoCake WP Forms** menu in your WordPress admin screen.

For basic uage, have a look at [plugin's website](https://nocake.io/nocake-wp-forms).
